--- layout: default title: Gerd Zellweger ---

I am a co-founder at Feldera, where we are revolutionizing data processing with incremental compute.

My work sits at the boundary of operating systems, databases, networks, and system software that has to deal with real hardware rather than idealized abstractions.

Previously, I was a researcher at VMware Research, where I led the research effort on the NrOS. I earned a Doctor of Sciences in Computer Science from ETH Zurich in 2017 after spending a lot of time hacking on the Barrelfish Project.
